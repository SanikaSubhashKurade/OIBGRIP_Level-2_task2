About The A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

Born : 15 Octomber 1931 in Rameswaram.

Died : 27 July 2015 in Shilong.

He was studied physics and aerospace engineering. He spent the next four decades as a scintist and science administrator.

He worked mainly at Defence Research and Development(DRDO).

Also worked at Indian Space Reasearch Organisation(ISRO).

He was inimately involved in India's civilian space programme and military missile development efforts.He thus came to be known as "Missile Man".

His work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology.

He also played a pivotal organisational,technical and political role .

Kalam was elected as the 11th president of India in 2002.
